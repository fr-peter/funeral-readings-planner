Juuji — gospel cross glyph
==========================

File shipped: public/fonts/juuji-cross.woff2 (~2 KB)

This is a single-glyph subset of the "Juuji" typeface by Luc Devroye,
containing only the cross glyph, remapped onto U+2720 MALTESE CROSS and
shrunk/shifted for use before the Gospel introduction.

Source:
  https://luc.devroye.org/maltesecrosses.html
