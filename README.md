# Funeral Readings Planner

*A resource for planning & printing Catholic funeral readings in Canada.*

A small, self-contained web app that helps you choose the readings for a Catholic funeral liturgy and produce a clean, print-quality PDF of the selected readings. The readings are taken from the liturgical books approved for use in Canada.

## Features

- **Search for readings**: The lectionary has a total of 100 funeral readings, psalms, and Gospels to choose from. Featuring keyword search and semantic search (allowing you to search by themes and meaning), you can easily find readings relevant to you.
- **Quality typesetting in your browser**: Using OpenType features and a custom drop-cap solution, you can produce high-quality printouts for the ambo.
- **Save and share**: Keep a named library of funerals, export/import them, and share an entire Liturgy of the Word as a single link. All data is stored on your device—nothing is stored remotely on a server. When sharing, the whole Liturgy of the Word is encoded in the link itself, so it is readable by anyone who receives it.
